Spotlight on Monopoly Deal

MONOPOLY DEAL keeps everything that makes MONOPOLY fun, and condenses all of the frantic action into a fast-paced card game.

  • Monopoly Deal is the card version of the classic Monopoly game.
  • Collect sets of properties with different colors.
  • Earn and swap properties and charge other players rent.
  • Steal properties or demand money from other players when you get the right Action card.
